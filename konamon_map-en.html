Customer Engagement Challenge

  • Low Engagement: Reliance on traditional paper maps and advertising results in high customer acquisition costs and a lack of data insight to understand customer consumption behavior and preferences.
  • Disconnection: The separate processes of online information gathering and offline consumption experience create a disconnection between online and offline (O2O).

Core Solution: "Konamon Map"

This is an application that integrates map search with gamification. It seamlessly connects online information gathering with the offline consumption experience, transforming consumer behavior into a rewarding, continuous interaction.

Key Features of Konamon Map

The core value of the "Konamon Map" is to be an ecosystem that connects consumers and local businesses and online and offline experiences.

1. Map and Information Integration

  • Intuitive Map Search: Stores can be intuitively discovered using pins on an interactive map.
  • Multi-dimensional Filters: Allows users to filter stores by criteria such as rating, distance, and visited status. Each pin includes detailed information like business hours and user reviews.

2. Gamification and Loyalty

  • Stamp Rally/Achievements: Users earn digital stamps by checking in or making purchases at stores, which unlock NFT badges or special discounts (SDG 8, 11).
  • Ranking and Competition: Users are ranked based on the number of unique stores visited or the amount spent, fostering a sense of competition and encouraging them to explore the region.

3. Data Feedback and Business Support

  • Heatmap Generation: Aggregates stamp rally data to generate heatmaps of visitor traffic, providing local businesses with valuable data on under-visited areas and peak times.
  • Direct Feedback Loop: Provides real-time data on customer preferences and behavior to the businesses, allowing for timely menu adjustments and targeted promotions.

Konamon Map Impact
