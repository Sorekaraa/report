Challenge and Solution

The shortage rate for non-regular employees in the food and beverage industry reaches 70%, making sudden staff absences and peak-time coverage major challenges. To solve this, we will build a talent sharing platform based on **"Common Training" and "Certification System."**

1. Introduction of Common Training Program (SDG 8 & 9)

  • Common Training: Basic training (food hygiene, customer service, POS operation) and mastery of **standard Konamon recipes** via e-learning.
  • Effect: Achieves standardization of education, contributing to the reduction of training costs (approx. **30% reduction** in other company cases).

2. Introduction of "Konamon Part-Timer Certification" (SDG 8 & 11)

  • Certification System: Assessment combining a written exam (hygiene, service, POS) and a practical exam (cooking demo, role-play, register operation) to **certify immediately employable personnel**.
  • Effect: Provides incentives like wage increases to certified individuals, improving motivation and retention rates.

3. Building the Talent Sharing Platform

Certified personnel are shared among cooperative stores in the region during peak hours or in case of staff shortages. An IT system optimizes shift management and matching.
