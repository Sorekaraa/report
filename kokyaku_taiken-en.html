Problem: Low Customer Retention and Visitor Experience

The core challenge is the high customer acquisition cost of traditional advertising methods and the low re-visit rate after the initial consumption experience. We propose integrating an **interactive "Konamon making experience"** to increase customer value (SDG 11).

Three Pillars of Experience Improvement

① Introduction of the "Hands-on Konamon Experience"

  • **Service**: Offer a hands-on experience (e.g., making one's own takoyaki/okonomiyaki).
  • **Effect**: Increases customer stay time and the emotional value of the visit. It shifts the primary profit source from food consumption to **experience fees**.

② Tourist (Inbound) Support

  • **"During the Trip"**: Multi-language menu & cultural commentary via QR codes.
  • **"Post-Trip"**: Promote re-visitation through experience commemoration NFTs and SNS posting.
  • **AI Translation & SNS Support Tool**: Reduce the burden on individual stores through automatic translation and posting templates.

③ Creative Elements

  • "Your First Takoyaki" project.
  • "Memory Notebooks" and "Konamon Passports" to promote re-visitation.
  • Strengthen SNS appeal with "Photo Spots x Hashtag Posting."

Expected Results

Indicator Expected Value
Average Customer Spend +20–30% (Introduction of experience fees/limited sets)
New Customer Segment Increase in Foreigner / Family Visitor Ratio
Customer Satisfaction Increased satisfaction from a "fun, educational, and shareable" experience
Repeat Rate Promoted re-visitation via experience stamps / passport system
