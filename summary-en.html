Project Problem Analysis and Solutions

This project systematically addresses the main challenges facing the local restaurant industry: high costs, labor shortages, and low customer engagement.

Identification of Key Challenges

  • High Costs: Crucial to reduce raw material and energy costs through joint purchasing/contracting.
  • Labor Shortage: High training costs and lack of capacity to handle peak demand.
  • Customer Engagement: High customer acquisition costs with traditional advertising and low customer retention.

Overall Project Value

The core value is to build an ecosystem that connects consumers and local businesses, and online and offline experiences, through three pillars: "Cooperative Groups," "Talent Sharing," and "Customer Service."

Solution Primary Effect SDGs Contribution
Cooperative Groups Cost reduction, stable procurement SDG 8 (Economic Growth)
Talent Sharing Reduced training costs, securing skilled staff SDG 8, 9 (Decent Work, Industry)
Customer Service Low-cost customer acquisition, increased repeat rate SDG 8, 11 (Sustainable Cities)

Contribution to Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

This project contributes significantly to SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth) and SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities). Cost reduction through Cooperative Groups and employment stability through Talent Sharing support the sustainable growth of the local economy.
